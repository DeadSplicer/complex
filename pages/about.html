EXAMPLE TEXT: Fox River Machining and Fabrication was founded in 1948 as Fox River Tool Co. Inc. The company has been owned by one family, the Cooper’s, since its inception. Lynn Cooper ran the company for many decades expanding the business started by his father and becoming one of the premier job shop machine companies in Northeast Wisconsin. From the very beginning, the company’s focus has been providing superior customer service in not only in providing quality OEM parts but also living up to its commitments in providing on-time delivery with 100% inspection of all parts not only to Fortune 500 corporations but also small to medium firms. This tradition has continued to the present as the company expands its presence geographically and into new industries throughout the United States.
